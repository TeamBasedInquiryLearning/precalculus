Activity 4.3.7.

Now use synthetic division, to find the quotient and remainder for the given rational function. \(f(x)=\frac{x^3+2x^2-3x+4}{x-2}\)
(a)
What is the quotient?
  1. \(\displaystyle x^2-3\)
  2. \(\displaystyle x^3+2x-3x+4\)
  3. \(\displaystyle x^2+4x+5\)
  4. \(\displaystyle 5\)
(b)
What is the remainder?
  1. \(\displaystyle 14\)
  2. \(\displaystyle -10\)
  3. \(\displaystyle 5\)
  4. \(\displaystyle 6\)
