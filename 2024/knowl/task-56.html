Which values are solutions to the absolute value equation \(3\lvert x-7 \rvert +5= 11\text{?}\) It may be helpful to rewrite the equation to isolate the absolute value.
  1. \(\displaystyle x=7\)
  2. \(\displaystyle x=-9\)
  3. \(\displaystyle x=0\)
  4. \(\displaystyle x=9\)
